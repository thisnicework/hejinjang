Gumieongmaek as Embodied Practice:
Autobiography of Dissolving

Soft Rehearsal for Fugitive Gathering is a lecture performance in which He Jin Jang stages an auto-ethnographic inquiry into the ethics of invisibility. Drawing from Korean shamanic traditions, sensory anthropology, and her own diasporic body, Jang proposes the "soft rehearsal" — not as preparation for something to come, but as an ongoing mode of collective sensing.

The performance weaves between lecture, ritual gesture, and live score to ask: what does it mean to gather without being gathered? How do sensing bodies practice refusal — and at what cost?

Premiered at the Immodern Medicines Symposium, RASA, Singapore, July 2025.
